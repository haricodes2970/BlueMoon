# User Personas & User Research

**Status: Draft v1 — authored 2026-07-22, hypothesis personas, pending
validation research and founder review**

## Research Method Note

These personas are currently **hypothesis-driven**, derived from the
gap identified in the [Literature Survey](./literature-survey.md), not
yet from interviews or surveys. They should be validated (or replaced)
with real user research before being treated as ground truth. Track
that validation work as an active task in
[CLAUDE.md](../../CLAUDE.md).

## Persona 1 — "First-Contact Fatima"

- **Context:** Meets people in professional or semi-formal settings
  (conferences, coworking spaces, classes) and wants to exchange contact
  without handing over a phone number to someone she just met.
- **Need:** Start talking immediately; decide later whether the contact
  is worth keeping.
- **Primary journey:** [Ephemeral session → optional contact save](./user-journey-and-flow-specification.md#journey-1-ephemeral-first-contact)

## Persona 2 — "Privacy-conscious Priya"

- **Context:** Actively avoids apps that require phone numbers or read
  contact lists. Currently uses Signal but finds onboarding friends onto
  it difficult.
- **Need:** Strong privacy guarantees without requiring the other person
  to already be "in the system."
- **Primary journey:** [Ephemeral session → optional contact save](./user-journey-and-flow-specification.md#journey-1-ephemeral-first-contact)

## Persona 3 — "Coordinator Alex"

- **Context:** Frequently needs to spin up short-lived group
  coordination (splitting a bill with people just met, organizing a
  one-off group activity) without creating a permanent group chat that
  outlives its purpose.
- **Need:** Fast group session creation, clean session end, no lingering
  group chat clutter.
- **Primary journey:** [Group session lifecycle](./user-journey-and-flow-specification.md#journey-2-group-session-lifecycle)

## Non-Persona (explicitly out of scope for V1)
